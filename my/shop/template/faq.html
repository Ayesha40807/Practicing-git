FAQ Set One

Get to know us better with this introduction section. You can always contact us if you need any further assistance.

How do I log in to the Mediguard web application?

You can log in by visiting the Mediguard website and entering your username and password on the login page.

Is the Mediguard web application compatible with all devices?

Yes, the Mediguard web application is designed to be responsive and accessible on various devices including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, you can use the 'Forgot Password' option on the login page to reset it. An email will be sent to your registered email address with instructions for resetting your password.

How does the AI-based chatbot assist users?

The AI-based chatbot offers support by providing accurate information and proactive guidance on medication management and healthcare queries, accessible directly within the application.

Can I generate reports on patient medication adherence?

Yes, you can generate reports on patient medication adherence using the 'Total Count Reports' feature in the dashboard, providing valuable insights into patient treatment compliance.

How can I set medication reminders?

You can set medication reminders by accessing the 'Reminder System' feature in your profile settings, where you can specify the medication, dosage, and frequency for reminders.
